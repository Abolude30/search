What is this?

CC Search is a tool that provides quick and direct access to Creative Commons licensed content.

The metadata our licenses provide allow content providers easy ways to search within their libraries for licensed content.

With CC Search, we give you direct access to the freely licensed content available from many of the world's top content providers.

Learn more about how this works.
